What is E-Sports?


E-Sports also known as Electronic Sports is played all around the world in a competitive setting using video games. Competitive video games is becoming an international phenomenon with billions of dollars up for grabs as well as millions of fans watching live or online. Online streaming platforms such as Twitch and Youtube have allowed casual gamers to become serious celebrities and stars who can earn up to seven-figure earnings and many brand endorsements
